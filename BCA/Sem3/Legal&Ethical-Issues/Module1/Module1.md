# Definition of Law
law is a set of rules and principles  that govern the humand conduct in a civil society are closely related as the law changes with the changes in the society.

# Branches of Law
## International Law
 - International law governs the relation between the nations and international organizations.
## Constitutional Law
 - it defines the structure of Govt. and fundamental rights of citizens.
## Criminal Law
 - It deals with offences against the state and society
## Civil Law
- it focuses on disputes between individual business or institutions rather than with criminal offences. Business law is an integral part of civil law.
## Business Law
 - it governs trade, industry and commerce.
# Scope of business Law
- Business law deals with rights and obligations arising out of transctions between commercial persons commercial person means an individual, a partnership or a company carrying a business. the following acts are included within the scope of business law:
  - Contract Act 
  -  Sales of Good Act
  - Partnership Act
  - Negotiable Instrument Act
  - Insolvency  Act
  - Companies Act
  - Insurance Act
  - Arbitration Act
  - Patent & Copyright Act
